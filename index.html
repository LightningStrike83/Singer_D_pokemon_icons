Row 2 Pokeballs

  • Pokeball

    A standard Pokeball, sold for 200P.

  • Great Ball

    1.5x greater catch rate than the standard Pokeball.

  • Ultra Ball

    2x greater catch rate than the standard Pokeball.

  • Premier Ball

    A bonus for buying 10 or more Pokeballs all at once.

Row 3 Pokeballs

  • Master Ball

    The only model of Pokeball with a 100% catch rate.

  • Dusk Ball

    A specialized Pokeball with a 4x catch rate during the night or in a cave.

  • Quick Ball

    A specialized Pokeball with a 4x catch rate when thrown on the first turn only.

  • Dive Ball

    A specialized Pokeball with a 4x catch rate for Pokemon encountered when surfing, diving, or fishing.

Row 4 Pokeballs

  • Friend Ball

    A special Pokeball made from apricorns; Pokemon caught in this ball have their base happiness increased.

  • Dream Ball

    A special Pokeball given by Fennel or made from apricorns; Pokemon from the Dream World come in this ball. When used to catch a sleeping Pokemon, has a 4x catch rate.
